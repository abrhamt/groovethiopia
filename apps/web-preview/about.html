    — ABOUT

    From Addis,
    to the world.

    Groovethiopia is a private members collective and creative house based in Addis Ababa. We operate at the intersection of culture, craft, and commerce — producing flagship events, trading in rare objects, and curating hospitality experiences for a discerning international audience.

    Founded in 2019, the company grew out of a simple belief: that Ethiopia's cultural moment deserves infrastructure built to the highest international standard. Not imitation of elsewhere — but the real thing, here.

    From the underground Shukshuta series to the flagship Horizon Festival, from rare collector vehicles to a private members club in the making — every project shares the same editorial line: Curating the New Horizon.
